<p><p>Fiber Optic Rotary Joints (FORJ) are devices that allow the transmission of optical signals between stationary and rotating structures, facilitating communication in applications such as robotics, aerospace, and medical equipment. The market encompasses two main types: Single-Channel FORJs, which transmit optical signals through a single fiber, making them suitable for applications with minimal data requirements. In contrast, Multi-Channel FORJs can transmit multiple fiber signals simultaneously, catering to complex systems that require higher data throughput and enhanced communication capabilities.</p></p>
