<p><p>The tire machinery market consists of various types of equipment, including extrusion and cutting machinery. Extrusion machinery is vital for shaping rubber compounds into continuous forms, which are then processed into tire components. It ensures consistent thickness and quality. Cutting machinery, on the other hand, is used to accurately slice rubber sheets and other materials into specific shapes and sizes required for tire production. Together, these machines enhance efficiency, precision, and quality in the tire manufacturing process.</p></p>
