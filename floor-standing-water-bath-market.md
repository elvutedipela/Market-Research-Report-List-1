<p><strong>The Floor-standing Water Bath Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating Water Bath</li><li>Non-Circulating Water Bath</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor-standing water bath market consists primarily of two types: circulating water baths and non-circulating water baths. Circulating water baths maintain a uniform temperature by using pumps to circulate water, ensuring consistent heating for experiments or procedures. Non-circulating water baths, on the other hand, rely on the natural convection of water, providing simpler designs and lower costs but with potential temperature variations. Both types serve essential roles in laboratories and industrial applications, catering to distinct needs in temperature control.</p></p>
