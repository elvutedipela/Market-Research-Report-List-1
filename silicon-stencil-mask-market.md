<p><ul><li>UV Nanoimprinting</li><li>Thermal Nanoimprinting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Stencil Mask market encompasses two primary types: UV Nanoimprinting and Thermal Nanoimprinting. UV Nanoimprinting utilizes ultraviolet light to create nanoscale patterns on substrates, offering high resolution and efficiency, making it ideal for applications in electronics and optics. Thermal Nanoimprinting, on the other hand, employs heat and pressure to imprint patterns onto materials, providing excellent conformity and durability. Both techniques are pivotal in the fabrication of advanced devices, driving innovation in semiconductor and nanotechnology sectors.</p></p>
