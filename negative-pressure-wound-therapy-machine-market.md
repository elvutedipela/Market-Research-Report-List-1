<p><ul><li>Continuous Negative Pressure</li><li>Discontinuous Negative Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) machines are categorized into two main types: Continuous Negative Pressure and Discontinuous Negative Pressure. Continuous negative pressure devices maintain a constant vacuum, promoting consistent drainage and blood flow to enhance tissue healing. In contrast, discontinuous negative pressure devices operate intermittently, applying suction in cycles to create a more dynamic healing environment, which may facilitate skin growth and reduce discomfort. Both types aim to improve wound management and accelerate recovery.</p></p>
