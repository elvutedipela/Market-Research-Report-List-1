<p><strong>The Desiccants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Absorption</li><li>Chemical Absorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desiccants market is categorized into two main types: physical absorption and chemical absorption. Physical absorption involves materials that attract moisture through surface adsorption, typically involving substances like silica gel or clay. These desiccants can be reused after regeneration. In contrast, chemical absorption involves a chemical reaction that binds moisture permanently, often using compounds like calcium chloride. This type typically has a higher moisture capacity and is ideal for environments with high humidity levels, providing effective moisture control across various applications.</p></p>
