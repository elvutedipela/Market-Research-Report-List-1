<p><p>The plastic geogrid market is categorized into two main types: uniaxial stretch and two-way stretch geogrids. Uniaxial stretch plastic geogrids are designed to withstand tensile forces in one direction, making them ideal for soil reinforcement in embankments and retaining walls. In contrast, two-way stretch plastic geogrids provide multidirectional strength, enhancing stability and load distribution in applications such as road construction and pavement systems. Both types contribute to improved soil stability and efficient construction practices.</p></p>
