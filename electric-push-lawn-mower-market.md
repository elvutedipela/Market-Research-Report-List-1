<p><p>The electric push lawn mower market is divided into two main types: corded and cordless models. Corded electric mowers are powered through a plug, offering continuous runtime and reliability, ideal for small to medium-sized lawns. However, they may limit mobility due to the need for an extension cord. On the other hand, cordless electric mowers are battery-operated, providing freedom of movement and convenience without the hassle of cords. They are suitable for various lawn sizes but may require battery management to ensure adequate power.</p></p>
