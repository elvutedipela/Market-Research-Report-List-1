<p><p>The refrigerating machine oils market is primarily divided into two types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oils are derived from refined crude oil and are commonly used in traditional refrigeration systems due to their cost-effectiveness and good lubrication properties. Synthetic oils, on the other hand, are artificially produced and designed to offer superior thermal stability, lower viscosity, and better performance in extreme conditions. This differentiation helps cater to varying industry needs, influencing efficiency and lifespan of refrigeration systems.</p></p>
