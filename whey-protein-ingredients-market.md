<p><p>The whey protein ingredients market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC)**: Contains 70-80% protein, providing a rich source of nutrients and bioactive compounds. </p><p>2. **Whey Protein Isolate (WPI)**: Offers 90% or more protein with minimal fats and carbohydrates, ideal for those seeking high purity. </p><p>3. **Hydrolyzed Whey Protein**: Pre-digested for faster absorption, beneficial for post-workout recovery. </p><p>4. **Demineralized Whey Protein**: Reduced mineral content, suitable for specific dietary needs or formulations. Each type caters to distinct consumer preferences within the nutritional supplement market.</p></p>
