<p><p>The polycarbonate (PC) resin market is categorized primarily into two types: Phosgene type and Non-Phosgene type. The Phosgene type involves the polymerization of bisphenol A (BPA) with phosgene, resulting in high-performance materials suitable for various applications. In contrast, the Non-Phosgene type utilizes more sustainable methods, often employing alternative processes that do not require phosgene, leading to eco-friendly production options. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, including automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
