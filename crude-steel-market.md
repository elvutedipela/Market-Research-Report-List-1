<p><p>The crude steel market can be categorized into three types based on deoxidation levels. Fully deoxidized steel exhibits minimal oxygen content, enhancing its properties and suitability for high-stress applications. Semi deoxidized steel contains moderate oxygen levels, balancing strength and machinability for various uses. Not deoxidized steel retains higher oxygen content, which can impact its quality but may lower production costs. Each type serves different industrial demands, influenced by specifications for strength, ductility, and processing requirements.</p></p>
