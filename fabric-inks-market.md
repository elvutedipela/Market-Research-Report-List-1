<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fabric inks are essential for textile printing, categorized into various types based on their chemical compositions and applications. Reactive dye inks bond with fabric fibers, providing vibrant colors and excellent wash durability, making them ideal for cotton and other cellulosic materials. Acidic inks are mainly used for protein-based fibers like silk and nylon, delivering bright colors and high wash fastness. Paint inks, on the other hand, offer versatility across diverse fabrics, delivering a durable finish but may require more preparation and curing.</p></p>
