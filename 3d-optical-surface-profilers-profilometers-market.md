<p><p>3D Optical Surface Profilers, or profilometers, utilize advanced optical techniques to measure surface topography with high precision. The market includes White Light Interference profilometers that capture surface features by analyzing interference patterns from reflected light, offering high vertical resolution. Confocal technology, on the other hand, employs a focused laser beam and pinhole detection to provide high-resolution imaging of surface structures, enabling detailed 3D mapping. Both technologies are vital in industries demanding precise surface analysis, such as semiconductor manufacturing and materials science.</p></p>
