<p><p>The oil-based lubricants market is primarily divided into two main types: natural and synthetic lubricants. Natural lubricants are derived from organic sources, such as vegetable oils and animal fats, offering eco-friendly options that often provide good lubrication and biodegradability. In contrast, synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered for enhanced performance, offering superior stability, high-temperature resistance, and reduced wear, making them suitable for demanding applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, manufacturing, and industrial machinery.</p></p>
