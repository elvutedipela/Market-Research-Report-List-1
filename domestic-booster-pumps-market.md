<p><p>The domestic booster pumps market features two primary types: single-stage pumps and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps operate with one impeller, providing a simple and cost-effective solution for low to moderate pressure needs in residential applications. In contrast, multi-stage pumps utilize multiple impellers, allowing for higher pressure and flow rates, making them suitable for larger homes or situations requiring elevated water pressure. Both types enhance water supply efficiency, catering to various household demands and preferences.</p></p>
