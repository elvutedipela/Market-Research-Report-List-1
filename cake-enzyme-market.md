<p><p>The cake enzyme market can be segmented into three main types: Fungal Alpha-amylases, Maltogenic Alpha-amylases, and Others. Fungal Alpha-amylases are derived from fungi and play a crucial role in breaking down starches, enhancing dough texture and moisture retention. Maltogenic Alpha-amylases, sourced from malt or other grains, are specialized in maintaining softness and shelf life by preventing staling. "Others" encompass additional enzymes and additives that support various baking processes, improving flavor, color, and overall product quality.</p></p>
