<p><p>Lung cancer treatment markets include several modalities, each serving specific patient needs. Surgery primarily involves tumor removal, beneficial in early-stage cancers. Chemotherapy utilizes potent drugs to kill cancer cells, often applicable in advanced stages. Radiotherapy targets tumors with high-energy rays, effective for localized treatment or palliative care. Photodynamic therapy employs light-sensitive drugs activated by light to destroy cancer cells, while laser therapy uses focused light to remove or shrink tumors. Each approach plays a vital role in comprehensive lung cancer management.</p></p>
