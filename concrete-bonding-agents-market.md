<p><p>Concrete bonding agents are crucial for enhancing adhesion in construction and repair applications. The cementitious latex-based market offers flexible, durable solutions that improve the bond strength between concrete layers, making them ideal for overlay applications. In contrast, the epoxy-based market provides strong, chemical-resistant bonds, suitable for high-stress environments and heavy-duty repairs. Both types cater to different needs, with latex-based agents focusing on flexibility and ease of use, while epoxy products emphasize strength and durability in demanding conditions.</p></p>
