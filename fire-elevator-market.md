<p><strong>The Fire Elevator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split Type</li><li>Straight Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire elevator market can be categorized into two main types: split type and straight type. Split type fire elevators feature separate shafts for the lobby and service areas, enhancing safety and efficiency during emergencies. Conversely, straight type fire elevators have a single shaft design, providing direct access between floors without separation. Each type is tailored for specific building needs, with the split type often being preferred in larger structures to minimize risk and optimize evacuation processes.</p></p>
