<p><p>The vehicle floor mat market comprises two main types: flat floor mats and molded floor mats. Flat floor mats are typically universal and can fit a range of vehicle interiors, providing basic protection against dirt and wear. In contrast, molded floor mats are custom-designed to fit specific vehicle models, offering superior coverage and durability. Molded mats often feature raised edges and deep grooves to trap dirt and water, enhancing functionality while contributing to the overall aesthetic of the vehicle's interior.</p></p>
