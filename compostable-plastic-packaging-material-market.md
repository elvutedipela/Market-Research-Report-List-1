<p><p>The compostable plastic packaging material market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **PBAT (Polybutylene Adipate Terephthalate)** - a biodegradable polymer offering flexibility and strength. </p><p>2. **PBS (Polybutylene Succinate)** - known for its excellent biodegradability and heat resistance. </p><p>3. **PLA (Polylactic Acid)** - derived from renewable resources like corn starch, known for its rigidity and transparency. </p><p>4. **PHA (Polyhydroxyalkanoates)** - produced by microbial fermentation, it's fully biodegradable. </p><p>5. **Starch Blends** - combining starch with other polymers for better performance. </p><p>6. **Others** - includes various innovative materials and composites.</p></p>
