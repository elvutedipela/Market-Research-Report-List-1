<p><ul><li>Semi-Positive Displacement (SPD) Type</li><li>Centrifugal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submersible grinder pumps market features two primary types: Semi-Positive Displacement (SPD) and Centrifugal pumps. SPD pumps utilize a combination of a rotating impeller and a stationary cutter to grind solids and facilitate efficient liquid movement, making them ideal for handling tough waste materials. Centrifugal pumps, on the other hand, use rotational energy to move fluids and are typically more efficient in lower-viscosity environments. Both types are essential for managing wastewater and are commonly used in residential and industrial applications.</p></p>
