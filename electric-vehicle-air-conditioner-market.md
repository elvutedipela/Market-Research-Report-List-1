<p><p>The electric vehicle air-conditioning market comprises mainly two types: hybrid-driven and motor-driven systems. Hybrid-driven systems utilize the vehicle's internal combustion engine to generate power for the air-conditioning unit, optimizing energy efficiency during operation. In contrast, motor-driven systems rely solely on electric motors to operate the air-conditioning, enhancing performance and cooling efficiency, especially in fully electric vehicles. Both types aim to improve passenger comfort while minimizing energy consumption, thus contributing to the overall efficiency of electric vehicles.</p></p>
