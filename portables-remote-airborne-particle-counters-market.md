<p><p>The Portables Remote Airborne Particle Counters market is divided into two main types: ionizing and non-ionizing particle counters. Ionizing counters utilize radioactive sources to detect particles, offering high sensitivity and accuracy, often used in stringent environments like clean rooms and laboratories. Non-ionizing particle counters use laser or light scattering technology, making them safer and more user-friendly, suitable for various applications such as indoor air quality monitoring and industrial settings, where real-time data is essential for ensuring compliance and safety.</p></p>
