<p><p>The genealogical DNA test market consists of three primary types: Y-DNA tests, which trace paternal lineage through the Y chromosome and are exclusive to males; mtDNA tests, which explore maternal lineage through mitochondrial DNA inherited from mothers; and autosomal DNA tests, which analyze a combination of DNA from both parents to create a comprehensive family tree across multiple generations. Each test serves a unique purpose in genealogical research and appeals to different consumer needs for ancestry exploration.</p></p>
