<p><p>The 3D Time-of-Flight sensor market comprises several measurement types, including Continuous Wave Measurement, Pulse Measurement, and Others. Continuous Wave Measurement utilizes a constant light signal to determine distance, offering high accuracy and precision in applications like automotive and robotics. Pulse Measurement, on the other hand, emits short light pulses and measures the time taken for reflection, making it effective for both short and long distances. The "Others" category includes hybrid systems and unique methods tailored for specific applications, enhancing versatility in the market.</p></p>
