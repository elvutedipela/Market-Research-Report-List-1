<p><p>The medical tablets market can be categorized into two primary types: daily use and operation use. In contrast, operation use tablets are administered in clinical settings, often as part of anesthesia or post-operative care, to facilitate procedures or manage pain. Both segments play crucial roles in patient care and treatment efficacy within healthcare systems.</p></p>
