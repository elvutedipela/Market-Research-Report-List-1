<p><p>The motorized actuator market consists of two primary types: stepper motors and servo motors. Stepper motors are used for precise control in applications requiring accurate positioning and repeatability. They offer incremental movement, making them ideal for robotics and 3D printers. On the other hand, servo motors provide high torque and speed, making them suitable for dynamic applications like robotics and automation. Both types of actuators enhance efficiency and precision in various industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
