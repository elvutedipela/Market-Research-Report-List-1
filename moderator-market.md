<p><p>Moderator market types are crucial in nuclear reactors, affecting the efficiency and control of nuclear fission. Graphite moderators provide a low neutron absorption cross-section, supporting sustained reactions, while heavy-water moderators, using deuterium oxide, enhance the neutron economy and allow for natural uranium fuel usage. Other moderators include light-water, which is widely used due to its availability, and various experimental materials like beryllium or molten salts. Each type influences reactor design, safety, and operational efficiency in different ways.</p></p>
