<p><strong>The Mitral Valve Intervention Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Replacement Product</li><li>Repair Product</li><li>Annulus Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mitral Valve Intervention Market comprises three main product types: Replacement Products, Repair Products, and Annulus Products. Replacement Products involve surgical or transcatheter procedures to fully replace the malfunctioning mitral valve. Repair Products focus on techniques that mend or restructure the existing valve to restore function, such as sutures or clipping devices. Annulus Products target the supportive ring structure of the valve, enhancing stability or reshaping it to improve mitral valve function and overall heart performance.</p></p>
