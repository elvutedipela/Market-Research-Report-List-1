<p><p>The interlayer films market comprises three primary types: EVA, PVB, and SGP interlayer films. EVA (ethylene-vinyl acetate) films are known for their flexibility and adhesion, commonly used in automotive and architectural applications. PVB (polyvinyl butyral) films provide excellent optical clarity and safety features, primarily utilized in laminated glass. SGP (sentry glass plus) films offer superior strength and impact resistance, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Each type caters to varied industries, focusing on safety, durability, and aesthetics.</p></p>
