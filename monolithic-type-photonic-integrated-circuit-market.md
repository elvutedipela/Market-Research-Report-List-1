<p><strong>The Monolithic Type Photonic Integrated Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit</li><li>Active Photonic Integrated Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The monolithic type photonic integrated circuit market is divided into two main types: passive and active photonic integrated circuits. Passive photonic integrated circuits do not require external power to operate; they utilize components like waveguides and filters to manipulate light signals. In contrast, active photonic integrated circuits incorporate elements such as lasers and modulators that require external energy to perform functions like signal generation and amplification. Both types serve critical roles in telecommunications, data processing, and sensing applications.</p></p>
