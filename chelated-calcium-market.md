<p><p>The chelated calcium market comprises various types that enhance calcium absorption in the body. Calcium orotate aids in cellular transport, while calcium aspartate is known for its bioavailability. Calcium lactate is gentle on the stomach and suitable for all ages. Calcium citrate has a higher solubility, making it ideal for those with low stomach acid. Calcium malate combines calcium with malic acid, promoting energy production, whereas calcium gluconate is often used in intravenous applications for rapid calcium replenishment.</p></p>
