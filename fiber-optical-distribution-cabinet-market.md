<p><p>The Fiber Optical Distribution Cabinet (FODC) market is categorized into single cabin and double cabin types. Single cabin cabinets are compact, housing a limited number of fibers and suited for smaller installations or environments where space is constrained. In contrast, double cabin cabinets accommodate larger fiber counts, making them ideal for extensive network deployments requiring higher capacity and management capabilities. Both types ensure efficient fiber management, protection, and connectivity, catering to the varying needs of telecommunications and data transmission sectors.</p></p>
