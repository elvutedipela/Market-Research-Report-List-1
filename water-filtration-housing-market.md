<p><p>The water filtration housing market includes various types, each serving specific purification needs. Reverse osmosis systems eliminate contaminants through a semi-permeable membrane, ensuring high purity. Ozone water purifiers utilize ozone gas to disinfect and eliminate bacteria, offering chemical-free treatment. Activated carbon filters absorb impurities and improve taste through adsorption. Distillers purify water by boiling and condensing vapor, effectively removing dissolved solids and pathogens. Together, these technologies cater to diverse consumer demands for clean and safe drinking water.</p></p>
