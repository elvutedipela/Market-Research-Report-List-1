<p><p>Glass Fibre Reinforced Plastic (GFRP) is a composite material made by combining glass fibers with a plastic matrix, offering enhanced strength and durability. The GFRP market is divided into two main types: thermosetting plastic products, which cure through a chemical reaction and provide excellent heat resistance, and thermoplastic plastic products, which can be remolded upon heating and offer greater flexibility. Both types are widely used in industries like construction, automotive, and aerospace, driven by their lightweight and corrosion-resistant properties.</p></p>
