<p><p>The pleated membrane filter market comprises various types, including PP (Polypropylene), PES (Polyethersulfone), PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), and Nylon filters, each catering to specific applications. PP filters are known for their chemical resistance and affordability, making them suitable for liquid filtration. PES filters offer superior flow rates and are ideal for sterile applications. PTFE filters excel in capturing fine particles and are effective in harsh environments, while Nylon filters are versatile and often used in aqueous and organic liquid filtration. Other specialty filters cater to specialized needs.</p></p>
