<p><p>The hybrid combiners market includes two main types: 90-degree and 180-degree hybrid combiners. </p><p>90-degree hybrid combiners are used to combine or split signals in wireless communication systems, allowing for efficient power handling and isolation between channels. </p><p>180-degree hybrid combiners, often used in phased array antennas, provide phase shifting capabilities, enabling optimal signal interference and improved performance in applications like radar and satellite communications. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing signal quality and system efficiency.</p></p>
