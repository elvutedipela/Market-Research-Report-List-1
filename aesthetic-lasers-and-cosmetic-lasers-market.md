<p><strong>The Aesthetic Lasers and Cosmetic Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ablative Lasers</li><li>Non-Ablative Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aesthetic and cosmetic laser market is categorized into two main types: ablative and non-ablative lasers. Ablative lasers remove the outer layers of skin, promoting significant skin resurfacing, which is effective for scars, wrinkles, and skin irregularities. In contrast, non-ablative lasers penetrate the skin without damaging the surface, stimulating collagen production and tightening skin with minimal downtime. Both types are widely used in various cosmetic procedures, catering to different skin concerns and patient preferences.</p></p>
