<p><p>Cut-resistant clothing for sports is categorized based on the ANSI cutting levels, which indicate the fabric's resistance to cutting hazards. ANSI levels A1-A3 denote lower protection suitable for sports with minimal cut risks, like cycling or jogging. Levels A4-A5 offer medium protection, ideal for activities involving sharp equipment, such as rock climbing. Levels A6-A9 provide high-level resistance, essential for intense sports with higher cut hazards, such as ice climbing or extreme winter sports, ensuring safety without compromising performance.</p></p>
