<p><p>Glycol ethers are versatile solvents used in paints, coatings, cleaners, and industrial applications. They are categorized into two main types: **P-series** and **E-series**. **P-series glycol ethers** are derived from propylene oxide, offering lower toxicity and milder odor, making them suitable for consumer products and environmentally sensitive applications. **E-series glycol ethers** are based on ethylene oxide, providing stronger solvency and higher performance in industrial applications but with greater toxicity concerns. Both types cater to diverse industries, balancing performance, safety, and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
