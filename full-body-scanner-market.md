<p><strong>The Full Body Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Ray Scanner</li><li>Millimeter Wave Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The full body scanner market consists primarily of two types: X-Ray scanners and millimeter wave scanners. X-Ray scanners use ionizing radiation to generate detailed images of the body, detecting concealed objects effectively but raising privacy and health concerns. In contrast, millimeter wave scanners utilize non-ionizing radio waves to produce 3D images, ensuring a safer approach without harmful radiation. Both types are widely used in airports and secure facilities for security checks, enhancing passenger safety while addressing privacy issues.</p></p>
