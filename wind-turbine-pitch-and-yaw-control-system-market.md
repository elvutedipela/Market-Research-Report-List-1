<p><p>The Wind Turbine Pitch and Yaw Control System market includes three primary types: Inline Version, Angular Version, and Combined Version. The Combined Version integrates both methods, enhancing performance and adaptability to varying wind conditions. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing energy production and improving turbine reliability in different operational environments.</p></p>
