<p><strong>The Biological Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autografts</li><li>Allografts</li><li>Xenografts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biological implants market includes three primary types: autografts, allografts, and xenografts. Autografts involve transferring tissue from one part of a patient's body to another, minimizing rejection risk. Allografts utilize tissues sourced from human donors, offering benefits for larger grafts but carrying potential immunological challenges. Xenografts involve using animal tissues, typically from pigs or cows, suitable for certain procedures but facing compatibility concerns. Each type serves specific medical needs, influencing treatment options in transplantation and surgical applications.</p></p>
