<p><strong>The Metal Decorating Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based Inks</li><li>Solvent-Based Inks</li><li>UV-Cured Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal decorating inks market comprises three primary types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are eco-friendly and suitable for various applications due to their low volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent-based inks offer excellent adhesion and durability, making them ideal for high-performance needs. UV-cured inks, which cure quickly under ultraviolet light, provide vibrant colors and superior resistance to scratches and chemicals, making them popular for premium metal decorating applications across various industries.</p></p>
