<p><p>The inspection microscopes market primarily features binocular and trinocular microscopes. Binocular microscopes provide a two-eyepiece design, allowing users to view specimens with stereoscopic depth perception, ideal for detailed inspections in various industries. In contrast, trinocular microscopes include a third eyepiece, enabling simultaneous viewing and digital imaging; this feature is particularly valuable for documentation and analysis. Both types are widely utilized in quality control, electronics, and biological research, enhancing precision in detailed examinations and fostering innovation in various applications.</p></p>
