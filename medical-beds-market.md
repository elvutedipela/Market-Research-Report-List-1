<p><p>The medical beds market comprises primarily two types: manual and electric medical beds. Manual medical beds require caregivers to adjust the bed's position using mechanical levers or knobs, providing flexibility at a lower cost. In contrast, electric medical beds are equipped with motorized systems allowing patients to adjust their position with ease, enhancing comfort and convenience. Electric beds are often preferred in hospitals and care facilities for their efficiency, while manual beds are common in home care settings due to affordability.</p></p>
