<p><p>The lung isolation device market comprises various types, including endobronchial blockers and lung dissociation kits. Endobronchial blockers are specialized devices inserted into the bronchus to isolate one lung, facilitating procedures like surgeries on the contralateral lung. Lung dissociation kits typically include tools and components designed to achieve lung isolation, improving surgical access and minimizing complications. Both types enhance the safety and efficacy of thoracic surgeries, catering to the needs of healthcare professionals in advanced medical settings.</p></p>
