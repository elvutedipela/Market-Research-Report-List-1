<p><p>The Direct Attach Cable (DAC) market consists of various types, primarily defined by their connectors and data rates. QSFP (Quad Small Form-factor Pluggable) supports high-speed connections for data centers, while SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) is widely used for shorter distances. CXP and Cx4 cater to high bandwidth applications. CFP and CDFP support up to 400 Gbps for advanced networking. Each type serves specific applications, providing flexibility and efficiency for interconnect solutions in telecommunications and data networking environments.</p></p>
