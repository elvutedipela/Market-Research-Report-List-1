<p><p>The capsule coffee makers market is divided into closed source and open source systems. In contrast, open source systems allow compatibility with various third-party capsules, promoting flexibility and cost-effectiveness. This market segmentation reflects diverse consumer preferences, with some valuing the convenience of closed systems while others prioritize versatility and the ability to choose from a wider range of coffee options.</p></p>
