<p><p>Direct drive rotary tables are precision devices used in various industries for automated positioning and machining. The External Rotation Direct Drive Turntable features an external motor that drives the platter, offering high torque and fast movement without belts or gears for minimal backlash. In contrast, the Internal Rotation Direct Drive Turntable incorporates a motor located within the table itself, allowing for a more compact design and improved thermal management. Both types enhance accuracy and reliability in applications such as CNC machining and robotics.</p></p>
