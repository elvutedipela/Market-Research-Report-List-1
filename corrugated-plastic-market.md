<p><p>Corrugated plastic, also known as twin-wall plastic or fluted plastic, is a lightweight, durable material made from high-density polyethylene or polypropylene. It consists of two parallel sheets with a fluted structure in between, enhancing its strength while keeping it lightweight. This versatile material is widely used across various industries, including packaging, advertising, construction, and agriculture, owing to its water-resistant, UV-stabilized, and recyclable properties.</p><p>The Corrugated Plastic Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period. Additionally, innovations in manufacturing processes and material formulations are leading to enhanced product performance, further boosting market adoption.</p><p>Current trends include a rising preference for corrugated plastic in the e-commerce sector due to its protective qualities and ability to withstand shipping conditions.
