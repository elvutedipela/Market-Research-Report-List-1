<p><ul><li>Powered Subwoofers</li><li>Passive Subwoofers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car subwoofer market primarily features two types: powered subwoofers and passive subwoofers. Powered subwoofers are self-contained units with built-in amplifiers, making them easy to install and efficient in delivering deep bass without requiring additional components. Conversely, passive subwoofers lack an integrated amplifier, necessitating an external amplifier for power. While passive subwoofers often offer greater flexibility in customization and sound quality, powered subwoofers are popular for their convenience, appealing to users seeking a quick and effective audio upgrade.</p></p>
