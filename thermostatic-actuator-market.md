<p><strong>The Thermostatic Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corded Control</li><li>Wireless Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermostatic actuator market is divided into two main types: corded control and wireless control. Corded control actuators connect directly to a central heating system via wires, providing reliable communication and consistent performance. In contrast, wireless control actuators use radio frequency or Wi-Fi technology, offering greater flexibility in installation and ease of use, as they eliminate the need for physical wiring. This allows for remote temperature adjustments and integration with smart home systems, enhancing user convenience and energy efficiency.</p></p>
