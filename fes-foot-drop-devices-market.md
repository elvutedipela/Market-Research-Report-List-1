<p><ul><li>Plantar Pressure Sensor Based</li><li>Tilt Sensor Based</li><li>Gyroscope & Acceleration Sensor Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FES foot drop devices market includes various types based on sensor technology. Plantar pressure sensor-based devices detect foot position and adjust stimulation to help lift the foot during walking. Tilt sensor-based devices utilize angle measurements to determine leg position and provide stimulation for a natural gait. Gyroscope and acceleration sensor-based devices use dynamic motion data to adapt stimulation in real-time, enhancing responsiveness. Each type aims to improve mobility and reduce the risk of falls for individuals with foot drop.</p></p>
