<p><p>The Ultra-High Definition Television market comprises three main types: LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) TVs use backlighting, providing good quality at affordable prices. LED (Light Emitting Diode) TVs are essentially advanced LCDs that utilize LED backlighting, offering better contrast and energy efficiency. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) technology delivers superior picture quality with vibrant colors and deep blacks by allowing individual pixels to emit light, making it the premium choice in the UHD market.</p></p>
